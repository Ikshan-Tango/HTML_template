So, to join us on this exhilarating tech journey, be a part of the recruitment process. The recruitment process would consist of 2 rounds: an aptitude test and a personal interview.

  • Aptitude test: The first step of recruitment is a series of questions ranging from aptitude to basic logic constructs. It is open to all first-year students only. No prior tech knowledge is required for the aptitude test.
  • Personal Interview: Students crossing the aptitude test will be qualified for the PI round, where we evaluate your motivation and ability to be a part of MLSC.
